Fire Stone (火之石)
Evolves Vulpix into Ninetales, Growlithe into Arcanine, Eevee into Flareon, and Pansear into Simisear when used.

Great Ball (超级球)
A high-performance Ball that provides a higher catch rate than a Poke Ball.

Leaf Stone (叶之石)
Evolves Gloom into Vileplume, Weepinbell into Victreebel, Exeggcute into Exeggutor or Alolan Exeggutor, Nuzleaf into Shiftry, and Pansage into Simisage when used.

Master Ball (大师球)
The best Ball with the ultimate performance. It will catch any wild Pokemon.

Moon Stone (月之石)
Evolves Nidorina into Nidoqueen, Nidorino into Nidoking, Clefairy into Clefable, Jigglypuff into Wigglytuff, Skitty into Delcatty, and Munna into Musharna when used.

Poke Ball
A device for catching wild Pokemon. It is designed as a capsule system.

Thunder Stone (雷之石)
Evolves Pikachu into Raichu or Alolan Raichu, Eevee into Jolteon, and Eelektrik into Eelektross when used.

Ultra Ball (高级球)
An ultra-performance Ball that provides a higher catch rate than a Great Ball.

Water Stone (水之石)
Evolves Poliwhirl into Poliwrath, Shellder into Cloyster, Staryu into Starmie, Eevee into Vaporeon, Lombre into Ludicolo, and Panpour into Simipour when used.
